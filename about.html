#StayInTouch

A place to organize and mange your outfits

This report discusses the result of the work done on the topic “Wardrobe”.
It is a part of the Advance Web Development project going in the Computer Science and Engineering Department, XCSE.
The development of an application framework for providing a common platform for students to manage their clothes and let them share pieces of clothing by giving an option that other people would be able to request for their clothes/ shoes.
